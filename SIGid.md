Signal Identification Guide

This wiki is intended to help identify radio signals through example sounds and waterfall images.
Most signals are received and recorded using a software defined radio such as the RTL-SDR, Airspy, 
SDRPlay, HackRF, BladeRF, Funcube Dongle, USRP or others.
